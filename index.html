WHO ARE WE?

World Class Programs is a collective of like-minded individuals who are passionate about cultural education. Teaching the future of the world is our passion and we work every day to improve. We recogize that in order for us to raise a considerate and world-conscious next generation, we need to focus on global education in high school. We bring the curriculum alive with valuable experiences and cultural immersion opportunities. Along with educational benefits, students will meet new people from around the world, and experience a new way of life different from their own. All of this combines to form the ultimate summer experience with World Class Programs.

OUR MISSION

Superior, eye-opening education is our goal and safety is our priority. Giving students the opportunity to travel abroad, experience new cultures, and gain a different perspective on the world is what we aim to do. There is a need for students to have teachers that aren't just passionate about learning but passionate about students. We tailor our programs each year to the students that attend our programs. Students fill out detailed questionaires about THEIR interests and THEIR goals for the summer. Our staff take these into account when developing the programs schedules. In order to maintain this unique and tailored experience across all programs, we keep our staff to student ratio 6:1 or better.

TELL ME ABOUT SAFETY

We offer programs around the world. Our program destinations are selected based off a few criteria: cultural richness, safety, and accessibility. Our goal on each program is to provide for our students a place to learn and grow safely. The ability for our students to be independent is contingent on the high safety standards we set in place. Because of this, World Class Programs performs routine safety checks in each program destination for six months leading up to the program date. Apartments are thoroughly vetted and all of our staff members receive background checks yearly. We only hire trusted education professionals from around the US and the world.

TYPICAL DAY

Each day you'll start with your major class that you've selected to attend five days a week from 9 until noon. After lunch, you'll either attend your minor class (Monday, Wednesday, and Friday) or you'll participate in an afternoon activity! Students will also have the option to attend an evening activity with the staff. These activies might include a concert, a night photography excursion, philosophy symposiums, karaoke, etc. After the evening activity, students will have dinner either with staff or with the new friends they've made abroad! On the weekends, students take day trips around their host country. Depending on the length of the trip and the program, students will sometimes stay overnight on these daytrips.
