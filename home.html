JOIN US AN AN ADVENTURE ACROSS THE WORLD

WHO ARE WE?

World Class Programs is a collective of like-minded individuals who are passionate about cultural education. Teaching the future of the world is our passion and we work every day to improve. We recogize that in order for us to raise a considerate and world-conscious next generation, we need to focus on global education in high school. We bring the curriculum alive with valuable experiences and cultural immersion opportunities. Along with educational benefits, students will meet new people from around the world, and experience a new way of life different from their own. All of this combines to form the ultimate summer experience with World Class Programs.

ABOUT US

OUR MISSION

Superior, eye-opening education is our goal and safety is our priority. Giving students the opportunity to travel abroad, experience new cultures, and gain a different perspective on the world is what we aim to do. There is a need for students to have teachers that aren't just passionate about learning but passionate about students. We tailor our programs each year to the students that attend our programs. Students fill out detailed questionaires about THEIR interests and THEIR goals for the summer. Our staff take these into account when developing the programs schedules. In order to maintain this unique and tailored experience across all programs, we keep our staff to student ratio 6:1 or better.
